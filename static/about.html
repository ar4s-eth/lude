The Problem Statement

Not every problem statement rises from a problem,
many just are ther to make your life better and beautiful.


Either every potential media consumer is on internet or in very near future would be.

With the ease of publishing content at media platforms
like YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook and many others..
the consumer economy has flourished for content creators
of all domains and technical expertise.

Podcast publishing has become a massive business vertical of its own.

Podcasts are primarily human conversations. And podcasters like to publish podcast video and clips for better reach.


An Idea

Luminous Decibels, give a picture to your words.


An easy way to generate a video for what you want to say.

A simple way that would allows someone who just knows how to fill online forms,
create an interesting video.


The Solution

OpenAI Whisper to transcribe. OpenCV/FFMpeg/PyAudio to transform media.


A platform that can convert provided audio or transcript into a Lyrical Video.

You record an audio or create a transcript.
The product will generate a video of required qualities for you to upload and share online.


What's Been Made

A web interface with three step process...


Upload your Audio, and Transcribe it.

Edit the transcript if required, choose a background template and Generate Video.

Play the generated online or Download it to publish elsewhere.

At GitHub: https://github.com/abhishekkr/lude
